Central Vista

The Central Vista at Keianza is more than just a beautifully designed corridor—it's the soul of the entire development. This grand central avenue is a thoughtfully curated landscape of symmetry, light, and life that connects the various thematic zones within Keianza while offering residents a multi-functional open space. It’s a space meant for movement, engagement, and contemplation all at once. Elegant seating areas, shaded resting pods, and subtle soundscapes make it a favorite retreat for residents seeking both calm and community.

With wide pedestrian walkways, ambient lighting, public art installations, interactive fountains, and tree-lined promenades, it acts as a social spine where morning jogs, weekend fairs, outdoor yoga sessions, and cultural gatherings unfold in harmony.
